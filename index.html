Niehku Mountain Villa is located in the Riksgränsen ski resort, just over 200 km north of the Arctic Circle. Up here in the north-westernmost reaches of Sweden, the landscape is wild, wondrous, and ever-changing.

  • The “Malmbanan” railway runs straight past Niehku, tracing the border between Sweden and Norway.
  • This legendary train line between Kiruna and Narvik has been transporting iron ore for over a century.
  • Niehku Mountain Villa is built on the site of the line’s old roundhouse, which operated from 1902 to 1923.
  • The next afternoon, you could be taking in sweeping views of Norway’s breathtaking fjords.
  • With the assistance from a helicopter we will take you to remote and seldom visited places.
